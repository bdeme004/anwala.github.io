100 Thieves recruits Teenage as second Apex Legends player

100T is on a signing spree.

Adam Fitch
Image via Respawn Entertainment

100 Thieves is quickly building up its Apex Legends roster, signing new player “Teenage” just a day after adding Connor “Gigz” White as its first competitor in the battle royale title.

Teenage previously was a H1Z1 player, a rank no. 1 Widowmaker in Overwatch, and is “currently tied for the Apex Legends kill-record with 41 kills,” according to the organization’s press release.

Apex Legends isn’t the only game that 100 Thieves has expanded into rapidly. The team, run by esports legend Matthew “Nadeshot” Haag, signed four players to its Fortnite roster in the past week.

There’s no word from developer Respawn Entertainment as to whether Apex Legends will receive an esports push, but that hasn’t stopped the community from competing. Events like Twitch Rivals and Code Red have already been hosted with prominent streamers and personalities taking part. One thing’s for sure though: That’s that 100 Thieves is prepared.
