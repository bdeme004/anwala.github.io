Sunnan e Nisai

Also known as Sunnan Nisai

Sunnan e Nisai, is one of the Kutub al-Sittah (six major hadith collections) of Sunni Islam. These prophetic traditions, or hadith, were collected by the Muslim scholar al-Nasai.

It contains more than 5,700 hadith collection and categorized into different books as below.
