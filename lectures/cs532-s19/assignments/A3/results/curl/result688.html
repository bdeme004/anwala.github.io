NAPOLI - Shock offer submitted to Mauro ICARDI

0 comments

Close to divorce from Inter Milan once and for all, Argentinian international striker Mauro Icardi (26), deprived of captain armband, and currently not playing, has reportedly been offered a 9 million euros annual salary deal (plus 2€m as benefits) by Napoli. Meanwhile, Juventus are planning to pitch in as well, and start a negotiation with former top exec of theirs, Giuseppe Marotta, current Inter CEO.
