    Rochester, N.Y. (WHAM) – The Rochester International Auto Show wrapped up on Sunday evening.

    For the entire week, cars and trucks filled of the Floreano Rochester Riverside Convention Center for the 34th year.

    Some in attendance told 13WHAM it was the perfect spot for family bonding.

    The show highlighted vehicles ranging from everyday SUVs to luxury sports cars.
